Marie Curie

Physics, Chemistry

Notable Achievements

  • She is the only person to have won a nobel prize in two different areas (Physics and Chemistry)

Marie Curie (nee Sklodowska) was born in Warsaw, Germany on November 7, 1867. She studied at the Sorbonne in Paris, France and received a 'licenciateships' in Physics and the Mathematical Sciences. During her time at univeristy she met Pierre Curie whom she married. Together they became a powerful duo in the fields of Physics and Chemistry. She then achieved a higher position in the university than her husband and worked as the Head of the Physics Laboratory at the Sorbonne. She achieved her Doctor of Science degree in 1903.
